# Chapter 15 Drill — Diagnose Your Own Anti-Pattern

## Steps
1. Copy the 5–10 patterns from Ch. 15 Part A you find most useful into your own notes.
2. Review your recent Claude Code history honestly. Find **one** anti-pattern from Part B you're guilty of.
3. Write the specific fix you'll apply next time, in one sentence.
4. Turn your single most-used pattern into a saved slash command or skill.

- Which anti-pattern is your *default* failure mode under time pressure? What early signal tells you it's happening?
- Restate the two-sentence summary of the whole course (Ch. 15 end) in your own words.
- Which Part A pattern, made into a command/skill, will save you the most time this month?
